NOTE:
1) The player, you, takes the first turn.
2) After that whomever is to play the next turn is decided randomly. So you may get 5 turns in a row, the computer may get 8 turns in a row.
3) If the computer gets more than one turn in a row, they all happen instantly. So when you press a button, your turn will play, and then the computers turn(s) will also play immediately.
4) The lists on the sides are so you can see how many turns the computer
took, and which actions it took.
5) The computers actions are chosen randomly as well.
6) The computer does not use the sword, flaming sword, or bandaid.
